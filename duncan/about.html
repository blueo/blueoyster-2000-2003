INSIDE THE BLUE OYSTER

The Blue Oyster Gallery is an artist run charitable trust whose mission is to run a financially viable arts gallery/project space that encourages the creation of contemporary, innovative and exciting art practices. We are committed to fostering, promoting and supporting the arts of Dunedin for the benefit of all the community - whatever their circumstances. The gallery was founded in June of 99 and has attracted artists through the exposure created by promotion, advertising, an extensive mailing list and public knowledge of the gallery. The Blue Oyster substains itself through public funding and the general good will of artists and the arts community as a whole.
