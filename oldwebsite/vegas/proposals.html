* * * PROPOSALS * * *

The Blue Oyster Gallery is an artist-run arts project space that promotes contemporary and innovative art practices in Dunedin, New Zealand. Since opening in 1999, the Blue Oyster Gallery has presented a range of emergent artists alongside artists of national and international significance. We have secured a unique position within the Dunedin art scene, nurtured links within a national network of contemporary arts projects and earned a reputation for showing experimental art practices. As a non-profit organization, our ethos is to shift the focus away from commodification, allowing artists the freedom to push the boundaries of their practice and produce work that is not commercially orientated. The Blue Oyster Arts Trust has created an environment that encourages audiences to engage in discourse regarding contemporary art issues.


* * * CALL FOR PROPOSALS * * *

The Blue Oyster Gallery is currently seeking proposals from artists and curators for our 2003 program.


* * * APPLICANTS SHOULD SUPPLY * * *
  1. An outline of the proposed project addressing conceptual basis, where appropriate, and a physical description
  2. Support material, including documentation that gives an indication of previous work
  3. A brief curriculum vitae


* * * THE BLUE OYSTER WILL PROVIDE * * *
  1. Promotion and awareness of the project through an extensive mail/email list.
  2. Distribution of fliers/press releases, local radio and media publications.
  3. Assistance with installation, organization and sourcing of materials.
  4. Full supervision of the project.
  5. No commission fees.


* * * THE ARTIST WILL PROVIDE * * *
  1. The gallery rental of NZ$240.00 if Creative New Zealand funding is not successful.
  2. Fliers and press release information two weeks prior to the preview for the mailouts.
  3. 100 stamps for mailing list distribution
  4. An opening function at 5.30pm on the Tuesday of the first week.
