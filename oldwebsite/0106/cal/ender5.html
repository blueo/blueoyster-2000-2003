Home Structure
Vanessa Crowe

Recent BFA graduate Vanessa Crowe toyed with the idea of structure, patterns, routine and the anomalies which are immanent between. She explored the strangeness in everyday living and dreaming and highlighted the relationships between order and chaos through a structure of multiple objects constructed from the salvaged remnants of other peoples homes: contemplating repetitive processes and domestic behaviors.
