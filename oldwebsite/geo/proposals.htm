The Blue Oyster is currently seeking proposals from artists and curators for our 2003 programme.
  
 
 
 
 
The Blue Oyster applies for annual funding from Creative New Zealand, which if successful will offer the artists free rental of the gallery and/or an artist fee. In our application to CNZ we will include part of the proposal and images from the successful applicants. Our adminstrator is available for any queries that you may have.
The proposal is also available in Acrobat PDF format for offline viewing/printing here. The offline version requires the Acrobat Reader program, available free from Adobe here.  
For further information please contact the Blue Oyster Gallery
