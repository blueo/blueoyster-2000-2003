Artist at the Blue Oyster
Ciaran Begley

Performance artist Ciaran Begley visited from Wellington and established an office space within the Blue Oyster Gallery. Anyone interested was encouraged to schedule an appointment with the artist within office hours. Begley used the gallery as a place in which to create, explore and develop ideas as well as using the exhibition as a whole to work on discourse, art making practice, installation and performance.
