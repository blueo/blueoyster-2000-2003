Leather Show
Blue Oyster 3rd Anniversary Group Show

Following the success of the Paper Show in 2000 and the Cotton Show in 2001, the Leather Show was a celebration of our 3rd Birthday as we continue with our themed anniversary series. The theme of leather incited some exciting and risky works from the following sixteen artists:



Rainy McMaster, Angela Singer, Bryony Allan, Nicola Farquhar, Iain Cheesman, Alannah Brown, James Robinson, Ana Terry, Sonja Van Kerkhoff, Ranitar Charitkul, Aidan Howse, Caroline McCaw, Bekah Carran, Lloyd Godman, Lisa Benson and Di Halstead.
